Why This Topic?

      The greatest part about this hobby is the joy I share with others. However, it just isn't the joy we receive when getting a gift such as this one, it is what it symbolizes. True Hope and Love, this is what we receive from the Lord and there is nothing greater than knowing this truth. Often we see the world as an evil place, the darkness surrounding us daily is huge! We are considered slaves to this darkness, and what better way to be set free from slavery than in the light of a living saviour!

John 8:12 says "I am the Light of the world, whoever follows Me will never walk in darkness, because you will have the Light of Life." - Jesus
